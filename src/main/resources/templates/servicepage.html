WHEEL ALIGNMENT

A four wheel alignment consists of adjusting the suspension of your vehicle so that all wheels are parallel to each other and perpendicular to the ground.

Three primary angles contribute to proper wheel alignment: camber, caster, and toe. Camber is the measure of the degree of perpendicular offset from the road surface. Caster is the angle of your wheel’s pivot points .Toe refers to the angle of directional difference between the tire and the centerline of the vehicle
